DELVE ✦ ROGUELIKE EDITION
⚰ MAIN MENU
Survive 7 floors. Collect items. Escape the dungeon.
❓ HOW TO PLAY

🎯 GOAL: Survive 7 floors and escape!

Floors 3, 6, and 7 have BOSSES. Other floors let you choose a path.

⚔ COMBAT

Attack, Defend (reduce next hit), or Use Potion (+8 HP)

Evasion % = SPD/(SPD+10)×100, capped 5–60%

Defense reduces incoming damage. Two-handed weapons block off-hand.


🗡 EQUIPMENT

1H Weapons: Dual wield — off-hand deals 60% damage

2H Weapons: Massive damage, locks off-hand slot

4 Armor Slots: Head · Chest · Pants · Boots

2 Accessory Slots with unique effects (BoI-style!)


💎 ACCESSORIES

Thorn Ring — Reflect 25% damage back to enemies

Vampire Fang — Heal 3 HP per kill

Lucky Coin — +1 to all damage rolls

Cursed Idol — RISKY: +4 damage but take more hits

Featherstone — Ignore all speed penalties

...and 9 more to discover!


💀 PERMADEATH: HP=0 = Game Over. No second chances.

💾 SAVE: Progress auto-saves to browser storage.
